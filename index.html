May 20, 2026 · Security Committee

Street Lighting Project Approved

After months of proposals, the municipality has approved funding for 12 new LED streetlights along Camino Cerro Chato and the connecting roads. Installation is expected to begin in July 2026. This was a key priority from last year's community survey.
